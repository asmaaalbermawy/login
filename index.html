WE ARE
FUTURE CITY.

We are a commercial painting company located in the heart of the Twin Cities metro area. We execute comprehensive paint services to improve building and portfolio performance, and we excel at delivering permanent results. 

Our clients are office, industrial and retail property managers or owners with property portfolios positioned in the Midwest.

Our unique abilities include building long-term, mutually profitable relationships with clients, applying expert knowledge to solve building integrity issues, dedicating ourselves to a high degree of professionalism and networking closely with industry partners.

So, what is a “future city”? To us, it is a statement of faith and a vision for what is to come. Learn more about our identity here.

OUR SERVICES

We offer a variety of services within seven core categories: exterior painting, interior painting, epoxy flooring, powerwashing, block sealing, tuckpointing & structural caulking and wallcovering.

As an APPROVED VENDOR by Sherwin Williams, Hirshfield’s and BASF, we are qualified to apply all products engineered and manufactured by these leading coatings suppliers.

The award-winning properties we serve speak for themselves.
